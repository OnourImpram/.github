# Code of Conduct

This account follows a professional, research oriented collaboration standard.

Participants are expected to:

1. Treat others with respect.
2. Discuss ideas with technical and scholarly rigor.
3. Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior.
4. Keep sensitive personal, clinical, academic, or security information out of public threads.
5. Report safety or security concerns through the appropriate private channel when disclosure could create risk.

Maintainers may moderate or remove comments that undermine safe, constructive collaboration.
